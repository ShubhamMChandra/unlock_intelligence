## Design notes for Gamma

- Clean, minimal, generous white space
- Matrix cells need to be large enough to write in with a pen (for the printed handout version)
- The framing lines go below the matrix, not above
- No branding heaviness — small logo if any
- Design for greyscale printing (standard office printer)
- The projector version and the printed version can be the same design — the projector version just gets filled in on screen while attendees fill in their paper copies
