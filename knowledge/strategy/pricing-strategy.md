## Context
Pricing intelligence gathered from 25+ competitor programs and B2B training market research, April 2026.

## Final Pricing (Decided April 2026)

Three agents (Growth, McKinsey, Startup 0-1) analyzed and debated. Option B (Balanced) was selected.

| Tier | Founding (Spring 2026) | Regular (Post-Founding) |
|------|------------------------|-------------------------|
| Individual | **$1,295** per person | $1,995 per person |
| Team (5-9 seats) | **$1,095**/seat | $1,595/seat |
| Team (10-19 seats) | **$995**/seat | $1,295/seat |

### Why Option B (not A or C)
- **Not Option A ($1,495 founding):** Only 25% off regular price — not meaningful enough for founding urgency. $200 closer to Revenue AI ($1,000) would have been fine, but the 33% increase to regular was less of a compelling "lock in now" argument.
- **Not Option C ($795 founding):** 88% increase to $1,495 regular is nearly impossible to execute. Risks attracting price-sensitive buyers who won't become advocates. The 0-1 advisor's logic was sound ("proof collection, not revenue event") but at $795 the premium positioning erodes.
- **Option B ($1,295):** 35% off $1,995 regular. Above $1,000 floor (maintains premium). $700 gap creates real urgency. Fits within individual professional development budgets ($1,000-$2,000/yr typical).

## Pricing Air Cover
Harvard validates the exact format (live, half-day sessions, no coding required) at $2,850+. This gives us the message: "University-caliber instruction at a fraction of executive education pricing."

## ROI Framing
"The average knowledge worker wastes 2.5 hrs/week on tasks AI could handle. At $75/hr, that's $9,750/year per employee. This program pays for itself in the first month."

## Display Strategy (Decided April 2026)
- **Temporal anchoring** (all 3 agents agreed — no strikethrough): "$1,295 per person / Founding cohort rate / Increases to $1,995 after Spring 2026"
- **No strikethrough pricing** — crossing out a price never charged is fiction; reads as B2C/Udemy energy in B2B
- **Both cards show pricing** — "custom pricing" kills conversion (37% longer sales cycles per B2B research)
- **"Most Popular" badge removed** from Team card — replaced with "Recommended for Organizations" (zero customers = nothing is "most popular")
- Team card shows "Starting at $1,095/seat" with temporal anchor and proposal CTA
