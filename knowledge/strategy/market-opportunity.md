## Demand Signals

- **86%** of companies increasing AI budgets in 2026 (Deloitte)
- **59%** of enterprise leaders acknowledge AI skills gap (Deloitte)
- **53%** educating broader workforce on AI fluency (Deloitte)
- **81%** of hiring managers prioritize AI skills (Microsoft)
- **56%** salary premium for AI-literate professionals (PwC)
- **Only 35%** have mature workforce-wide AI upskilling programs
- **85-95%** ILT completion rates vs. **5-15%** self-paced
